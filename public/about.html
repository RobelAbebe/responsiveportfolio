About Me

I'm an Ethiopian outstanding comedian, TV host and Fundraiser whose name has risen to stardom in the Ethiopian entertainment industry. I'm best known for my standup comedy and my youtube shows. Skills

I gain a lot of skills when i began my career as a radio program host in a popular radio drama called Yegna; which is a radio program funded by the UK and other sponsors. Afterward, i switched to performing 1 hour-long stand-up comedy. In my stand-up comedy, i presented humorous and informative political jokes, which appeals to the youth. I performed my comedy to different university students across the country. In my comedy, i ventilated the political activism and ethnic tensions in the country. Later on, i found a new role at EBS television hosting an interesting TV program named ‘Yebeteseb Chewata’. This role helped me to gain more skills and knowledge.
